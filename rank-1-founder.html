👤 Artist Profile & Executive Leadership

Official Biography

Chico Loco 40, born Samir Libari on October 16, 1999, is a New York-based international artist and music executive pioneering a revolutionary fusion of Arabic, English, and Spanish dark drill and trap music. As Co-Founder, Chief Operating Officer (COO), and Chief Technology Officer (CTO) of CL40 World LLC, he directs a global music syndicate managing worldwide publishing, rights distribution, and digital asset management. Operating from headquarters in New York with international operations in Morocco, San Francisco, and Sydney, CL40 World LLC stands as a leading independent record label and entertainment enterprise bridging strategic markets globally.

Professional Credentials & Authority

As an official Rights and Human Official associated with Google Flow Music, Amnesty International, and ASCAP, Chico Loco 40 combines artistic innovation with professional governance, ensuring ethical standards and international compliance across all CL40 World operations. His unique position as both creative visionary and technical infrastructure leader enables seamless integration of cutting-edge music technology with humanitarian principles.

Strategic Partnership

In partnership with LB0025, Chico Loco 40 and CL40 World LLC have established a formidable presence in the international music industry, operating as a full-service record label, publishing administrator, and digital distribution platform serving artists and content creators worldwide.
